User Profile Integration

To begin your journey toward enhanced emotional self-awareness, you will first create a user profile. This profile will serve as your personal space within the application, where you can track your emotional ratings, reflections, and progress over time. The user profile creation process involves:

  1. Providing basic information, including your name, age, and email.
  2. Selecting your emotional triggers from a predefined list.
  3. Setting your personal goals regarding emotional awareness and growth.
  4. Confirming your profile with a verification email for security.

Once your profile is set up, you can access tailored assessments and resources designed to meet your unique emotional needs.

Conditional Scenarios

Based on your emotional rating, you will be presented with scenarios that reflect your current emotional state. For example:

If your rating is below 5:

You might receive a scenario like: "You feel overwhelmed by a recent challenge at work. How would you respond?"

Options:

  • A) Take a break and clear your mind.
  • B) Discuss your feelings with a friend.
  • C) Seek guidance from a mentor.

After selecting an option, you will answer a follow-up question to explore your decision-making process.

Reflective Prompts

After engaging with the scenarios, you will have the opportunity to reflect on your emotional triggers. The application will present you with reflective prompts, such as:

  • What specific event triggered your emotional state today?
  • How did you respond, and how did it make you feel?
  • What would you do differently next time?

Answering these questions will deepen your understanding of your emotional patterns and help you develop strategies for managing them.

Additional Features

The Emotional Self-Awareness section also includes:

  • Progress Reports:

    Get weekly summaries of your emotional ratings and reflections to track your growth.
